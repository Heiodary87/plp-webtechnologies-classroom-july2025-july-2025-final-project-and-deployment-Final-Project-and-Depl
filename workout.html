Full Body Workout

  • Jumping Jacks – 30 sec warm-up, full body activation.
  • Push-ups – Upper body & core strength builder.
  • Squats – Targets glutes, thighs, and lower back
  • Plank – Builds endurance and strengthens your core

Arm Workout

  • Arm Circles – Improves mobility and tones shoulders.
  • Tricep Dips – Targets the back of the arms.
  • Shoulder Taps – Enhances balance and stability.
  • Bicep Curls – Use water bottles if no weights!

Leg Workout

  • Lunges – Works thighs and glutes.
  • Squats – Boosts leg power and flexibility.
  • Calf Raises – Tones calves and improves ankle strength.
  • Wall Sit – Burns and builds endurance.

Core Workout

  • Crunches – Strengthens abdominal muscles.
  • Leg Raises – Targets lower abs effectively.
  • Russian Twists – Engages obliques for a toned waist.
  • Mountain Climbers – Full-body workout focusing on core.
